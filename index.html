Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Moonton.
Released in 2016, it is designed for mobile devices and features fast-paced 5v5 battles where players choose from a variety of heroes with unique skills and roles.

The goal is to work with your team to destroy the enemy's base while defending your own. Mobile Legends has gained a large global following due to its easy-to-learn mechanics, quick matches, and competitive gameplay.
